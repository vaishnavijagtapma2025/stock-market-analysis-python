# Starter Ideas

These are project starting points proposed by the course team.

They are not approved charters yet. They are meant to help a team that wants a clear place to begin quickly.

Use them like this:

- pick one that feels interesting
- narrow it down for your own team
- verify the data in the first 24 hours
- rewrite it in your own words inside `CHARTER.md`

Do not copy the wording directly into your charter.

## Start Here

## What To Notice

- each idea already points to a likely stakeholder
- each idea gives you a likely metric and a simple baseline
- each idea tells you the first feasibility check to run
- each idea tells you the main risk before you get too far

The goal is not to remove your thinking. The goal is to make starting easier.
