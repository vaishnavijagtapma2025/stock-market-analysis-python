# Peer Samples

These are strong sample charters based on project ideas proposed by earlier student groups.

They are here to show you what a good charter looks like in practice:

- specific question
- clear stakeholder
- real numbers instead of vague promises
- realistic data plan
- honest scope limits

Read them as peer samples, not as templates to copy.

## What To Notice

As you read them, focus on these questions:

- Is the question narrow enough?
- Is the main metric clear?
- Is the success threshold a real number?
- Is the baseline simple and believable?
- Are the data sources named clearly?
- Do the scope limits protect the team from overclaiming?

## What Not To Do

- do not copy a topic just because it looks polished
- do not copy someone else's numbers
- do not copy someone else's data plan if it does not fit your question

Use these peer samples to understand the level of clarity expected.
